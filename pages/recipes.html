Chicken Tikka Masala

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Yogurt
  • Tomato
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Garam masala
  • Chili powder
  • Turmeric
  • Cumin
  • Coriander
  • Cream
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Lemon juice
  • Cilantro

Instructions

Marinate chicken in yogurt, lemon juice, and spices. Refrigerate for 1 hour.

Bake or grill chicken until cooked through.

Sauté onion, garlic, and ginger until soft.

Add crushed tomatoes, simmer until thickened.

Add chicken, heavy cream, and salt, simmer for 5-10 minutes.

Garnish with cilantro.

Serve hot with rice or naan.

Palak Paneer

Ingredients

  • Paneer
  • Spinach
  • Onion
  • Tomato
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Green chili
  • Garam masala
  • Turmeric
  • Cumin
  • Coriander
  • Cream
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Instructions

After the paneer cubes are golden brown, remove from the heat.

Ginger, garlic, onion, and green chilies should be sautéed until tender.

Cook the tomatoes until they are soft by adding salt, spices, and tomatoes.

Add the spinach and stir until it wilts.

Mix the blend until it's smooth.

Put the paneer and garam masala back in the skillet.

Add heavy cream and boil for ten to fifteen minutes.

If necessary, adjust the consistency and seasoning.

Serve warm with toast or rice.

Dal Tadka

Ingredients

  • Lentils
  • Onion
  • Tomato
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Green chili
  • Cumin seeds
  • Mustard seeds
  • Turmeric
  • Chili powder
  • Coriander powder
  • Garam masala
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Cilantro

Instructions

Lentils should be cooked until tender.

Ginger, garlic, onion, and green chilies should be sautéed till golden.

Cook the tomatoes until they are tender.

Add spices, mustard and cumin seeds to balance.

Simmer after adding the cooked lentils.

Add salt and butter and stir.

Add cilantro as a garnish.

Serve warm with toast or rice.
